# Future Features to Implement

As requested by the user, these following features should be implemented in the future when asked:

1. **Dual-Interface Access (Owner vs. Worker)**:
   - Separate login/interface for Owners and Workers.
   - Owner sees the current full app (billing, orders, analytics, income/expenses, etc.).
   - Workers can only see the customer name, measurements, and delivery date for their assigned orders.

2. **Automated WhatsApp Reminders**:
   - Send automatic WhatsApp messages to customers when their order status is changed to "Ready".

3. **Advanced Interactive Analytics**:
   - Implement deeper insights, such as Top Services (e.g., Saree Fall, Bridal Blouse) that generate the most revenue.
   - Customer retention and loyalty tracking (identifying top customers).
   - Export data functionality (e.g., PDF or Excel files of monthly income/expenses).

*(Note: Digital Catalog & Inspiration Gallery is already implemented in the 'exquisite tailoring app' catalog).*
